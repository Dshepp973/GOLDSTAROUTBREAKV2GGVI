GOLD STAR DISTRIBUTION RECALL CHECK
PUBLIC SAFETY GUIDE
README


What This Tool Is
This website helps you check whether an item you purchased may be affected by the Gold Star Distribution recall and tells you what to do next.
It is designed to:
* Reduce confusion

* Prevent accidental exposure

* Help families act calmly and quickly

* Point people to the right next step (safe / discard / check further)

________________


What This Tool Does
   * Walks you through simple questions

   * Helps you decide if an item is likely affected

   * Explains what to throw out and what may be safe

   * Helps you know when to contact a store or seek a refund

________________


What This Tool Does NOT Do
      * It does not replace FDA guidance

      * It does not diagnose illness

      * It does not provide medical or legal advice

      * It does not guarantee safety — it helps you decide responsibly

When in doubt, do not consume the item.
________________


How to Use
         1. Answer each question honestly

         2. Follow the result shown (Safe / Discard / Check Further)

         3. Check official FDA updates if unsure

         4. Contact the store if a refund is needed

________________


If Someone Feels Sick
If you or a family member feel unwell after eating a recalled product:
            * Seek medical advice

            * Mention possible exposure to a recalled product

            * Follow healthcare guidance

________________


Who Built This
This tool was created by V2G × Global Village Initiative (GVI) as part of a public education effort to make food safety information easier to understand and act on.
________________


Reminder
Food recalls happen to protect the public.
Using this tool is about care, not fear.
